Music Experiences: Introduction


Guide
Ideally this website serves not only as a straight up recommendation of specific songs/topics, more so a map to direct you onto paths of discovery in different branches of topics. These directions/reccomendations are generally more niche and obscure IMO, which is intentional.
You can start at a more comfortable/familiar point! press crlt f to find a specific topic/keyword. Or maybe just swipe and skim through to get an overview of the topics here! Personally I would try something exotic to kickstart the journey.
I am also making these as a form of tribute to what the past has molded me into currently, appreciatively.


How to use:
Using the inbuilt player on the website, you can play music from a playlist in the background. Or you could just use youtube no problem there...

Use the summary below to quickly check out all the music and navigate to them. Treat this as a map to explore different genres or styles of music. Remember to wear earbuds/headphones (especially noise cancelling ones). Also beware of "music fatigue" when you listen to too much of the same music (speaking from experience)
Get comfy in a cafe/ur room/in class and enjoy!

For the player, Privacy Enhanced Mode for the embedded videos are turned on, which means viewing them won't mess up your Youtube feed, so no need to worry bout that.


Tips and ideas:
Tip 1: Try using headphones(with/wo) noise cancelling or turning up the volume to hear the details in the music, especially in songs. Or try their instrumental versions. The experience was quite bizzare for me.
Tip 2: Sick of listening to your newest/regular loop of music? Try searching up some oldies/an old playlist to trigger some insane nostalgia.
E.g.: Young again, Arms of gold!!
Obviously, some of this music is meaningful to me just because my experience with encountering it. So before you listen to any, do check out the game/series associated with it, as that could be the ideal way to experience it.


Player functionality !!:
Player will open and contain embedded youtube video/playlist when you press on a music list title or item.
It has 3 toggles: Aspect-ratio, rigidity and position.
