Introduction


About this website
This website is a personal blog about me, my favourite books, music and content recommendations. These recommendations are not the most mainstream of content, so I hope that you can find some internet "gems" here. Such as obscure web series, really engaging and niche yt gaming series, a cathartic and therapeutic comedy show steeped in existentialism, a comfy enjoyment of relatable feels with a focus on daily life portrayed in silly fun, Inspiring euphoric and psychedelic experiences, and more.

If you are one that enjoys content perhaps possibly the same way I do, let me share with you these shards of wonder and beauty from the far corners and hidden corridors of the internet.


About Me
I am a casual gamer, avid Minecraft since-childhood fan, niche books binger and enjoyer of cute, cuddly stuff. I love sharing and discussing about hobbies and am about as approachable as a rock. My hobbies include gaming, reading books (mostly uh fiction I guess kinda), watching yt ofc, listening to music and making food I see on the internet (which I'm really bad at).

I like solitude and anonymity, going to quiet, private and secluded cafes and long videos. I like enjoying doing stuff on airplanes, cafes and while travelling, while listening to a way-too-wide-variety(not really) of music.

I kinda like the feeling of focusing on a specific highly enjoyable task/game and zoning out to it. Where your attention is tunnel visioned onto this specific thing, and everything else fades away.

I also like to enjoy long(~30min-2hours) videos while being comfy, wrapped tightly in bedsheets eating salty peppery popcorn, while lavishing in an air-conditioned room as heavy rain and/or thunderstorm provides the perfect ambiance. Did I mention I like, really like rain?

I guess I enjoy travelling as well. Being comfy, listening to music and having meals on trains or airplanes as the world zips by. The freshness of different places and environments.

Well, that's enough of me. Here's a summary of this website. Use it well to find, hopefully your new obsession or fascination.
